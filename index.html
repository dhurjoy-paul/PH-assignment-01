The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to create a modern, innovative nation by leveraging technology, sustainability, and education. It emphasizes digital transformation, renewable energy, skilled workforces, inclusive development, smart cities, and global partnerships to redefine its global identity and future.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future for cityzens.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
